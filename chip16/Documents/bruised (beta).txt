 About:

 Bruised is a fighting game.
 Beta version lacks certain features (eg. sound).
 
===========
 Controls:
===========
 
 LEFT - Move character left.
 RIGHT - Move character right.
 A - Punch.
 B - Kick.
 A+B - Block.
