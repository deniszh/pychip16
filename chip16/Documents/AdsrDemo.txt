 "AdsrDemo" for Chip16:
========================

 Date: 03/09/2012

========
 About:
========

 Demonstration of the capabilities with the
 newly added sound opcodes.

===========
 Controls:
===========

 UP, DOWN - Select a parameter to change.
 LEFT - RIGHT - Change the selected value.
 START - Play the generated sound.
