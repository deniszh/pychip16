==========================
 "MegaManX16" for Chip16:
==========================
 
 Author: Prads
 Date: 07/04/2012

========
 About:
========

 Mega Man inspired game for Chip16.
 The goal of the game is to kill the boss on the right side.

===========
 Controls:
===========

 D-PAD - Move player.
 Start - Start game.
 A - Shoot.
